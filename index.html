Enhancing Attendance Through AI-Augmented Workflows

A Strategic Approach for Hartley Brook Primary Academy

Prepared for Kay Godfrey, Regional Director at Astrea Academy Trust

Our Reality at Hartley Brook - Challenge & Opportunity

Current Position:

  • 91.2% overall attendance (vs. 95% target)
  • 29.4% Persistent Absence (~160 students)
  • Currently ranked last in attendance across the Trust
  • EYFS attendance at just 88%
  • ~14 cases of Severe Absence (<50%)

Our Context:

Serving Shiregreen: One of Sheffield's largest primaries in an area of high deprivation.

The Human Impact

Behind these numbers are children missing crucial learning opportunities every day. In a community where education already faces significant barriers, we cannot afford business as usual.

However, within this challenge lies our greatest opportunity for transformation.

Key Insight:

"Attendance is our number one barrier to improving outcomes for our children."

  • First-day calling 60-90 mins daily

  • Data entry and coding checks 45-60 mins daily
  • Sending repeated messaging 30-45 mins daily
  • Attendance tracking and analysis 60-90 mins daily

The Imbalance:

70% Admin Tasks

Our dedicated team spends the majority of their time on low-value administrative tasks rather than the high-impact family engagement that moves the needle on attendance.

The Bottleneck

With nearly 160 persistent absentees, this approach simply cannot scale to meet our challenge - particularly in early years where we're seeing the most concerning trends.

Enhanced Meeting Capture - Responsible Audio Recording

Key Benefits:

  • Staff fully present in meetings Better engagement with families
  • Complete capture of nuanced conversations No details missed during complex discussions
  • Rapid AI transcription and summarization Microsoft CoPilot transforms recordings into actionable notes
  • Richer documentation for CPOMs and attendance action plans Better evidence for interventions and safeguarding
  • Estimated time saving: 45-60 minutes per meeting Multiplied across dozens of meetings weekly

Responsible Implementation:

Clear consent protocols aligned with GDPR
Secure storage within Microsoft environment
Limited retention periods with automatic deletion
'Human-in-the-loop' review of all AI-generated summaries
Training for all staff on ethical recording practices
